Why Professional Pitching
Is Important

A poorly drafted CMA or project report can instantly result in application rejection. Banks require specific ratios, justified projections, and clear intent. A professionally drafted file maximizes your chances of high quantum and low interest rates.

FAQ

Quick FAQs

Credit Monitoring Arrangement (CMA) data is an analysis report of the past and projected financial performance of a company, rigorously required by banks to check creditworthiness for loans.

While we provide highly professional documentation to maximize your chances, the final loan approval strictly depends on the lending bank's policies and your CIBIL score.

A standard project report and CMA data usually take 3 to 5 working days depending on the complexity of your financials and data availability.
