Who Should File
GST Returns?

"If you are registered under GST, filing returns is legally mandatory—even if you had zero transactions this month."

Filing “Nil Returns” is essential to keep your registration active and avoid heavy penalties or account suspension. Our experts make this process seamless for you.

Eligibility Criteria

All Registered Businesses

Every entity with a valid GSTIN must file regular returns.

Proprietors / Individuals

Individuals providing taxable goods or professional services.

Freelancers & Professionals

Service providers crossing the prescribed turnover limits.

E-commerce Operators

Platforms (Amazon/Flipkart) collecting Tax at Source (TCS).

Input Service Distributors (ISDs)

Those distributing tax credits to multiple branches/offices.

Non-Resident Taxable Persons

Foreign individuals/firms supplying goods or services in India.

Regular Scheme

The standard scheme where you collect GST, pay tax on value addition, and can claim **Input Tax Credit (ITC)** on all business expenses.

  • Full ITC Claimable
  • Monthly/Quarterly Filings
  • Inter-state Sales Allowed

Composition Scheme

For small businesses with turnover up to ₹1.5 Crore. Pay a small fixed percentage of turnover (1% to 6%) with minimal compliance.

  • No ITC Claimable
  • Quarterly Tax Payment (CMP-08)
  • Limited Inter-state Sales
