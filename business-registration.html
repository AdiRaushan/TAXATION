Why It Matters

Why Should You Consider
Business Registration?

Legal Protection & Reputation

Incorporation gives your business its own legal identity, shielding personal assets and significantly boosting credibility with clients and partners.

Limited Liability

Protect your personal savings. Your responsibility for company debts is limited to your capital, ensuring financial safety for you and your family.

Better Funding Opportunities

Registered companies have a higher trust factor with banks. Gain easier access to business loans, credit lines, and institutional finance.

Attract Equity Investment

If you plan to scale, investors and VCs only invest in incorporated entities (like Pvt Ltd), making it essential for raising capital.

Documents Required for
Business Registration

Submit scanned copies only for digital processing. General documents required for LLP, OPC, Pvt Ltd, and Public Ltd.

Entity Structure

  • Private Ltd: 2 Directors & 2 Shareholders
  • OPC: 1 Director + 1 Nominee
  • Public Ltd: 3 Directors & 7 Shareholders
  • 2–3 Proposed Company Names (Preferential Order)

Director & Shareholder KYC

Mandatory for all

  • Pan Card (Mandatory)
  • Passport Size Photo
  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport / DL
  • Address Proof: Bank/Utility Bill (Not older than 2 months)
  • Details: Mobile, Email, Qualification, Place of Birth

Office & Legal

  • Latest Electricity/Utility Bill for Office
  • Rent Agreement & NOC (if Rented) or Ownership Proof
  • Police Station Details (Nearest Jurisdiction)
  • Draft MoA, AoA & Business Object Details
  • Authorised & Paid-up Capital Structure
