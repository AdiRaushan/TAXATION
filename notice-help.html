Common Demand Types

Income Tax Notice Assistance & Reply Services

Every notice has a specific purpose. Identification is the first step to resolution.

143(1)

Intimation

Received after ITR filing for arithmetical errors or mismatch in deductions vs proofs.

Required Docs

  • • Form 16 / 16A
  • • Investment Proofs
148

Escaped Income

The AO believes some income escaped assessment. Requires a highly technical response.

Required Docs

  • • Bank Statements
  • • Asset Purchase Deeds
245

Refund Set-off

Notice to adjust past unpaid demands against your current year's tax refund.

Required Docs

  • • Past Demand History
  • • Challan Copies
147

Re-assessment

Complete opening of past assessments based on new evidence or findings by the Dept.

Required Docs

  • • Full Audit Report
  • • P&L Statement

Response Deadlines:
Why Timing Matters

Income Tax and GST laws are strict about timelines. Missing a deadline doesn't just mean a fine; it means losing your right to defend yourself.

Normal Scrutiny (143/148)

Typical Window

Typical Window

GST ASMT-10 / DRC-01

Typical Window

What Happens if Missed?

Ex-Parte Assessment

The officer passes an order based on their assumption, usually resulting in the highest possible tax demand.

High Penalties

Penalties for non-compliance can be massive, reaching multiples of the tax sought to be evaded.

Bank Account Freeze

Department can issue attachment notices to your bank, blocking all withdrawals until the demand is satisfied.

  • GST Sales/Purchase Register
  • TDS Certificates

  • Property Purchase Deeds
  • Mutual Fund Statments
  • Business Invoices
