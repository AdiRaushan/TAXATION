Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 08, 2026

At MY TAXATION, we recognize the importance of your privacy and are committed to protecting the financial and personal data you share with us.

1. Information Collection

We collect information necessary to provide tax and financial services. This includes:

  • Personal Identity (PAN, Aadhaar, Name).
  • Contact Information (Phone, Email, Address).
  • Financial Records (Bank statements, income details, business records).
  • KYC documents required for government registrations.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Preparing and filing Income Tax Returns.
  • Processing GST and business registrations.
  • Communicating updates regarding your filings.
  • Providing financial consultancy as requested.

3. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, secure servers) to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your sensitive documents are handled only by authorized experts assigned to your case.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with outside parties, except for:

  • Government Portals (Income Tax, GST, MCA) as part of the filing process.
  • Instances where disclosure is required by law.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Client Consent

By using our services, you consent to our privacy policy and the collection of data as described above.

Data Concerns?

If you wish to request the deletion of your data or have security concerns, reach us at mytaxationonlychoice@gmail.com
